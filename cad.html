My Experience with Computer Aided Design

In our Digital Fabrication classes, we learnt how to use CAD (Computer Aided Design) applications, mainly Fusion 360. The application can be used to design objects that are able to be 3D printed or lazer cut. To apply what we had learned in class, we had used Fusion 360 to design and lazer cut the main chasis of our final project.

Fusion 360

During our lessons, we learnt about how to use Fusion 360. This included the basic functions such as creating a sketch, drawing rectangles and extruding planes in our design.
