FabLab Safety and Precautions

Before gaining access to the machines in FabLab, I had to learn about its rules and precautions. The rules include:

Attire

1. Wearing the appropriate attire of long pants, a short-sleeved t-shirt and covered shoes with no loose apparel such as necklaces.

2. Long hair must be tied up.

3. Hands must be free of adornment

Precautions

1. The use of machines in FabLab require a lecturer in charge to be present

2. In the event of emergency, keep in mind the important hotlines like the SP Emergency Hotline (6772-1234) or the Ambulance (995)

3. Do not play around in the area and make sure that the machines are used properly.

4. Any hot objects found in the machine to be removed using gloves.

5. Do not lean on the machines when in use.

6. Make sure the work-piece is secured onto the machine properly before starting the machine.

7. Keep in mind all emergency exits in case of a fire breakout

8. Keep in mind all locations of the emergency first-aid kits in case of any accidents when using the machines.
